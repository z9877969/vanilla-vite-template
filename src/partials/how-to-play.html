How to Play

  1. Take customer orders and prepare their meals as quickly as possible.
  2. Cook, plate, and serve dishes accurately to earn tips and rewards.
  3. Upgrade your kitchen appliances and unlock new recipes for better efficiency.
  4. Manage time wisely to keep customers happy and avoid losing orders.
  5. Unlock and explore new restaurants with unique challenges and cuisines.
  6. Participate in special events and daily challenges to earn extra rewards.
